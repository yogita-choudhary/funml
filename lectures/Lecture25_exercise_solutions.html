Lecture 25: Data and Label Efficient Learning (Active Learning)

Predicted probabilities:

\[p(y|x_1)=[0.80,\;0.10,\;0.10],\quad p(y|x_2)=[0.34,\;0.33,\;0.33]\] \[p(y|x_3)=[0.50,\;0.49,\;0.01],\quad p(y|x_4)=[0.60,\;0.20,\;0.20]\]

Solutions:

  1. Entropy for \(x_1\) and \(x_2\):

    \[H(x)=-\sum_{c=1}^{3} p_c \ln(p_c)\]

    For \(x_1\): \[H(x_1)=-(0.80\ln0.80+0.10\ln0.10+0.10\ln0.10)\] \[\approx -(0.80(-0.2231)+0.10(-2.3026)+0.10(-2.3026))\] \[\approx 0.64\] \[\boxed{H(x_1)\approx 0.64}\]

    For \(x_2\): \[H(x_2)=-(0.34\ln0.34+0.33\ln0.33+0.33\ln0.33)\] \[\approx -(0.34(-1.0788)+0.33(-1.1087)+0.33(-1.1087))\] \[\approx 1.10\] \[\boxed{H(x_2)\approx 1.10}\]

    \[\boxed{x_2\text{ has higher entropy.}}\]

  2. Least Confidence: \(LC(x)=1-\max_c p_c\)

    \[LC(x_1)=1-0.80=0.20\] \[LC(x_2)=1-0.34=0.66\] \[LC(x_3)=1-0.50=0.50\] \[LC(x_4)=1-0.60=0.40\]

    Largest \(LC\) means least confident: \[\boxed{x_2\text{ is selected first (LC = 0.66).}}\]

  3. Margin Sampling: \(M(x)=p_{(1)}-p_{(2)}\)

    \[M(x_1)=0.80-0.10=0.70\] \[M(x_2)=0.34-0.33=0.01\] \[M(x_3)=0.50-0.49=0.01\] \[M(x_4)=0.60-0.20=0.40\]

    Smallest margin is most uncertain: \[\boxed{x_2\text{ or }x_3\text{ (tie at margin }0.01\text{).}}\]

  4. Early vs late stage strategy:

    In fusion-based active learning:

    • Early stage emphasizes diversity-based selection to represent the global distribution.

    • Later stage emphasizes difficulty-based selection near decision boundaries.

    \[\boxed{\text{Early stage: diversity-based selection}}\]

Final answers: \[\boxed{H(x_1)=0.64,\;H(x_2)=1.10,\;\text{LC picks }x_2,\;\text{Margin picks }x_2/x_3,\;\text{Early: diversity}}\]
