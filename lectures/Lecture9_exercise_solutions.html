Lecture 9: Regularization and Performance Metrics

Given \[\bm{y} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad \hat{\bm{y}} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad N=3\]

\[\bm{\theta}= \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}, \qquad \|\bm{\theta}\|_2^2 = 5, \qquad \gamma=0.60\]

\[L(\bm{\theta}) = \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N(\hat{y}_i-y_i)^2 + \frac{\gamma}{N}\|\bm{\theta}\|_2^2\]

  1. Question: What is the residual vector \(\bm{r}=\hat{\bm{y}}-\bm{y}\)?

    • \(\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\\1\end{bmatrix}\)

    • \(\begin{bmatrix}0\\-1\\1\end{bmatrix}\)

    • \(\begin{bmatrix}1\\-1\\1\end{bmatrix}\)

    Solution: \[\bm{r}=\hat{\bm{y}}-\bm{y} =\begin{bmatrix}1-1\\1-2\\3-2\end{bmatrix} =\begin{bmatrix}0\\-1\\1\end{bmatrix}\] \[\boxed{(B)}\]

  2. Question: What is the Mean Squared Error (MSE)?

    • \(0.33\)

    • \(0.67\)

    • \(1.00\)

    Solution: \[\mathrm{MSE}=\frac{1}{3}(0^2+(-1)^2+1^2)=\frac{2}{3}\approx0.67\] \[\boxed{(B)}\]

  3. Question: What is the ridge objective value \(L(\bm{\theta})\)?

    • \(0.67\)

    • \(1.00\)

    • \(1.67\)

    Solution: \[\frac{\gamma}{N}\|\bm{\theta}\|_2^2=\frac{0.60}{3}\cdot5=1.00\] \[L(\bm{\theta})=0.6667+1.00=1.6667\approx1.67\] \[\boxed{(C)}\]

  4. Question: Suppose the third prediction changes from \(3\) to \(8\). Which metric would increase the most?

    • MAE

    • RMSE

    • MSE

    Solution: Squared error amplifies large mistakes the most. \[\boxed{(C)}\]

  5. Question: Two models have the same MSE, but Model A has much larger \(\|\bm{\theta}\|_2^2\). Which model does ridge regression prefer?

    • Model A (larger weights)

    • Model B (smaller weights)

    • Both equally

    Solution: Ridge penalizes large parameter magnitudes. \[\boxed{(B)}\]

  6. Question: If \(\gamma\) increases, what happens to the learned weights?

    • Weights grow larger

    • Weights shrink toward zero

    • Weights stay unchanged

    Solution: \[\boxed{(B)}\]
